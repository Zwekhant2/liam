Logan Paul to Buy Back NFTs from Fans After CryptoZoo Failure

January 10, 2024

Logan Paul has begun the process of buying back NFTs from fans who lost money in his failed cryptocurrency game.

The YouTube star announced the game CryptoZoo in 2021 but it never materialized, despite fans spending millions on game tokens.

Paul, 28, has now said he will buy back the NFTs, on the condition that fans who accept do not sue him.

This buy-back is a way for me to make whole those who intended to play CryptoZoo.

The buy-back is not intended to compensate those who gambled on the crypto market and lost.

Additional Information

Here is a list of key points:

  • The buy-back process will cost $2.3 million.
  • Players will be refunded 0.1 Ether per NFT.
  • The original sale was in September 2021.
